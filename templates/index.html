Welcome to Tunify

About Tunify

Tunify is a cutting-edge music discovery web app that integrates seamlessly with Spotify. With Tunify, you can dive deep into musical analytics, uncover hidden gems, and revisit the soundtracks of your life. Tunify tells you things that spotify wrapped won't tell you, and gives personalized music suggestions with the click of a button!

About Me

Hi there! My name is Zarif. I'm the creator of Tunify, a passionate music enthusiast and software developer dedicated to enhancing your listening experience. My passion and journey with music and coding has led me to develop this app to share the joy of music with you. I've worked on other web apps, but this is the project that I've been the most passionate about and spent the most time on. I utilized flask, a python microframework, alongside python and front-end languages to build this site for you.
